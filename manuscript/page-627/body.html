Chapter: 29

Rabindranath Tagore And I Compare Schools

"Rabindranath Tagore taught us to sing, as a natural form of self- expression, like the birds."

Bhola Nath, a bright fourteen-year-old lad at my Ranchi school, gave me this explanation after I had complimented him one morning on his melodious outbursts. With or without provocation, the boy poured forth a tuneful stream. He had previously attended the famous Tagore school of "Santiniketan"
